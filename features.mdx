# Features

Syra provides a comprehensive suite of trading intelligence features designed for traders, analysts, and developers in the Solana ecosystem. All features are accessible via Telegram, API, and autonomous agent workflows.

## Market Analysis

Real-time market data and analytics from multiple sources.

### Price & Volume Tracking

- **Multi-source price feeds** — Binance, CoinGecko, DEXScreener integration
- **Volume analysis** — 24h volume, trading activity, and flow metrics
- **Volatility indicators** — Track market volatility and price swings
- **Trend strength** — Identify bullish/bearish momentum across timeframes

<Info>
  Market data is aggregated from multiple sources to ensure accuracy and reduce single-point-of-failure risks.
</Info>

### Live Market Metrics

```json
    "volume24h": "$28.4B",

  }
}
```

### Trading Signals

AI-generated trading signals with entry/exit recommendations:

- **Entry price recommendations** — Optimal entry points based on technical analysis
- **Target levels** — Multiple take-profit targets with probability ratings
- **Stop-loss suggestions** — Risk management with calculated stop levels
- **Confidence scores** — AI-powered confidence ratings (0-100%)
- **Risk/reward ratios** — Clear R/R analysis for each trade setup

<Tip>
  Access trading signals via `/signal bitcoin` in Telegram or `GET /signal?token=bitcoin` via API.
</Tip>

## On-Chain Intelligence

Leverage blockchain data for deeper market insights.

### Smart Money Tracking

- **Net flow analysis** — Track smart money inflows and outflows
- **Holdings monitoring** — Monitor what smart wallets are accumulating
- **DEX trade tracking** — Real-time DEX transactions from notable addresses
- **DCA patterns** — Identify dollar-cost averaging by sophisticated traders

### Holder Analytics

- **Fastest holder growth** — Memecoins with rapid holder expansion
- **Whale behavior detection** — Unusual whale accumulation or distribution
- **Holder concentration** — Bubblemaps integration for holder distribution visualization
- **Smart money mentions** — Tokens mentioned by tracked smart money addresses

<Note>
  Smart money tracking uses Nansen integration and proprietary wallet classification algorithms.
</Note>

## AI Research & Insights

Deep research capabilities powered by AI and multiple data sources.

### Research Types

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Quick Research">

    - **Depth:** Surface-level analysis with key highlights
    - **Sources:** 5-10 sources

  <Tab title="Deep Research">

## Risk Analysis

Comprehensive risk assessment tools for informed decision-making.

### Rugcheck Integration

- **Token risk reports** — Comprehensive risk analysis for Solana tokens
- **Risk alerts** — Tokens with high risk scores (configurable thresholds)
- **Token statistics** — New, recent, trending, and verified tokens
- **Contract analysis** — Smart contract security assessment

### Risk Context

- **Risk/reward awareness** — Clear R/R ratios for every signal
- **Exposure considerations** — Position sizing recommendations
- **Scenario analysis** — Best/worst case outcome projections
- **Confidence levels** — AI-powered confidence ratings

<Warning>
  Always use Rugcheck integration to verify token safety before trading unknown Solana tokens.
</Warning>
