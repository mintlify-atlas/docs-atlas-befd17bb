# Syra for Traders

Syra provides traders with real-time intelligence, actionable signals, and risk management tools to make informed trading decisions in the fast-paced crypto markets.

## Quick Access via Telegram

The fastest way for traders to access Syra is through the Telegram bot:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start the Bot">
    Open [Syra Trading Agent Bot](https://t.me/syra_trading_bot) and press **Start**.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Check Supported Tokens">
    Use `/list` to see all supported tokens and their tickers.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Get Trading Signals">
    Request a signal: `/signal bitcoin` or `/signal SOL`
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Monitor News">
    Stay updated: `/news BTC` or `/top_mention today`
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Set up Telegram notifications to get alerts on major market moves or new signals for your watchlist tokens.
</Tip>

## Trading Signal Analysis

Syra's AI-generated signals provide comprehensive trade setups:

### Signal Components

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Entry & Exit">
    **Entry Price Recommendations**
    - Optimal entry zone based on technical analysis
    - Support/resistance levels consideration
    - Volume profile analysis

    **Take-Profit Targets**
    - Multiple TP levels (TP1, TP2, TP3)
    - Probability-weighted targets
    - Risk/reward optimization

    **Stop-Loss Placement**
    - Calculated stop levels below key support
    - ATR-based stop distance
    - Maximum loss percentage

    ```json

      "token": "BTC",
      "recommendation": "BUY",
      "entryZone": "$66,800 - $67,400",
      "targets": [

        { "level": "$75,800", "probability": 0.40 }

      "stopLoss": "$65,100",
      "riskReward": 3.2,
      "confidence": 78

    ```
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Technical Analysis">

    - **RSI (14):** Overbought/oversold conditions
    - **MACD:** Momentum and trend direction
    - **Moving Averages:** 50/100/200-day SMA/EMA
    - **Bollinger Bands:** Volatility and breakout zones
    - **Volume Profile:** Support/resistance based on volume

    **Pattern Recognition:**
    - Bull/bear flags and pennants
    - Head and shoulders patterns
    - Double tops/bottoms
    - Triangle consolidations
    - Divergences (bullish/bearish)

    <Info>
      All technical indicators are calculated using real-time data from Binance and DEXScreener.

  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Market Context">
    **Broader Market Analysis:**

    - **BTC correlation:** How the token correlates with Bitcoin
    - **Market regime:** Trending vs. ranging market identification
    - **Volatility state:** Current volatility vs. historical average
    - **Sentiment backdrop:** Overall market sentiment (fear/greed)

    **On-Chain Signals:**
    - Smart money accumulation/distribution
    - Whale wallet activity
    - Exchange inflows/outflows
    - Holder behavior patterns
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Risk Assessment">
    **Risk Factors Evaluated:**

    1. **Technical Risk**
       - Proximity to major support/resistance

## Risk Management Tools

Effective risk management is crucial for trading success:

### Position Sizing

<Steps>
  <Step title="Assess Signal Confidence">
    Higher confidence (greater than 75%) → Larger position size
    Lower confidence (less than 60%) → Smaller position or skip
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Calculate Risk per Trade">
    **Rule of Thumb:** Risk 1-2% of portfolio per trade
    
    Example: $10,000 portfolio, 2% risk = $200 max loss
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Determine Position Size">
    Position Size = (Max Loss) / (Entry - Stop Loss)
    
    Example: $200 / ($67,000 - $65,100) = 0.105 units
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set Stop-Loss">
    Always use the stop-loss level from Syra's signal. Never trade without a stop.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Portfolio Correlation

Avoid overexposure to correlated assets:

```bash
# Get correlation matrix
GET /binance/correlation-matrix

# Identify highly correlated pairs (>0.8)
# Diversify across uncorrelated or negatively correlated assets
```

<Warning>
  Never risk more than 2% of your portfolio on a single trade. Even high-confidence signals can fail.
</Warning>

## Advanced Trading Workflows

### Memecoin Trading Strategy

For traders focusing on high-risk, high-reward memecoin opportunities:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Discovery">
    **Find Early Opportunities:**

    1. **Fastest Holder Growth**
       ```bash
       GET /memecoin/fastest-holder-growth
       ```
       Identify memecoins with viral adoption.

    2. **Smart Money Mentions**
       ```bash
       GET /memecoin/most-mentioned-by-smart-money-x
       ```
       Follow what smart wallets are discussing.

    3. **Trending on X, Not DEX**
       ```bash
       GET /memecoin/trending-on-x-not-dex
       ```
       Catch social momentum before price pumps.
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Due Diligence">
    **Verify Safety Before Trading:**

    1. **Rugcheck Token Report**
       ```bash
       GET /token-report?address={contract_address}
       ```
       Check risk score (aim for less than 30 for lower risk).

    2. **Bubblemaps Holder Analysis**
       ```bash
       GET /bubblemaps/maps?address={contract_address}
       ```
       Visualize holder concentration (avoid if top 10 holders greater than 50%).

    3. **Developer Background**
       ```bash
       GET /memecoin/by-experienced-devs
       ```
       Prefer tokens by proven developers.
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Entry & Exit">
    **Execute with Discipline:**

    1. **Entry Strategy**
       - Wait for confirmation (volume, price action)
       - Scale in: 50% initial, 50% on confirmation
       - Never FOMO in at the top

    2. **Take-Profit Ladder**
       - TP1 (50% position): 2x return
       - TP2 (30% position): 5x return
       - TP3 (20% position): 10x+ (moon bag)

    3. **Stop-Loss Management**
       - Initial stop: -30% from entry
       - Move to breakeven after TP1 hit
       - Trail stop after TP2 hit
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Risk Controls">
    **Memecoin-Specific Risk Management:**

    - **Max allocation:** 5-10% of portfolio in memecoins total
    - **Per-token max:** 2% per memecoin
    - **Exit rules:** Take profits religiously, don't get greedy
    - **Survival test:** Prefer coins that survived recent dumps
       ```bash
       GET /memecoin/surviving-market-dumps
       ```

    <Warning>
      Memecoin trading is extremely high risk. Most memecoins go to zero. Only trade with capital you can afford to lose completely.
    </Warning>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### DeFi Trading Strategy

For traders focusing on established DeFi tokens:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Trend Following">
    **Identify Strong Trends:**

    1. Get signal for target token (e.g., Solana)
    2. Confirm trend with 50/100/200 SMA alignment
    3. Wait for pullback to key moving average
    4. Enter on bounce with tight stop below MA
    5. Trail stop as trend continues

    **Best for:** SOL, ETH, and major DeFi tokens with strong trends
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Mean Reversion">
    **Capitalize on Oversold Bounces:**

    1. Monitor RSI for oversold (less than 30) conditions
    2. Confirm with Bollinger Band lower touch
    3. Check sentiment for capitulation signs
    4. Enter near support with stop below recent low
    5. Target upper Bollinger Band or resistance

    **Best for:** Volatile altcoins in ranging markets
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Breakout Trading">
    **Trade Technical Breakouts:**

    1. Identify consolidation patterns (triangles, flags)
    2. Set alerts near breakout levels
    3. Wait for volume confirmation on breakout
    4. Enter on retest of breakout level
    5. Target measured move distance

    **Best for:** Tokens with clear chart patterns and catalysts
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Day Trading Workflow

Optimized daily routine for active traders:

### Pre-Market Routine (Before Trading)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Market Overview">
    Check overall market sentiment:
    ```bash
    GET /analytics/summary
    ```
    Review BTC/ETH trends, correlation matrix, smart money flows.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="News Scan">
    Get latest news for major tokens:
    ```bash
    GET /news?ticker=general
    GET /news?ticker=BTC
    ```
    Identify potential catalysts and risk events.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Watchlist Signals">
    Check signals for your watchlist:
    ```bash
    /signal SOL
    /signal ETH
    /signal BONK
    ```
    Note high-confidence setups (>70%).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Risk Check">
    Verify token safety for new positions:
    ```bash
    GET /token-report?address={address}
    ```
    Avoid tokens with Rugcheck score >50.
  </Step>
</Steps>
